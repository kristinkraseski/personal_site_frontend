About
I began my career in data at Long Island University, where I received a Masters Degree studying fish populations. I later attended the University of Georgia, where I received a PhD in hydrological modeling. I have a deep knowledge of the tools and programming languages used in data analysis and statistics, as well as experience in web development. In my most recent position, I was a senior Analyst and Program Coordinator at a large non-profit organization working to address environmental issues in the Northeast. Since then, I have completed a certification program in Business Data Analysis as a way to expand my knowledge of the needs and expectations around data in organizations and learn additional tools.
